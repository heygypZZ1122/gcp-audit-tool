        Controlled source hierarchy

        Regulatory Reference Library

        Read-only source register for the GCP computerised-systems track. Each source is labelled by authority and applicability so that contextual good practice is not presented as a direct legal requirement.

        ICH E6(R3)

        Good Clinical Practice — Final Guideline

        Section 4.3 covers computerised systems, including procedures, training, security, validation, system release, system failure, technical support and user management.

        Regulatory guideline Direct GCP applicability
        EMA/INS/GCP/112288/2023

        Computerised Systems and Electronic Data in Clinical Trials

        Primary EU GCP computerised-systems guideline covering validation, user management, security, audit trails, electronic data and system-specific controls.

        Regulatory guideline Direct EU GCP applicability
        FDA 21 CFR Part 11

        Electronic Records; Electronic Signatures

        Cited only where an FDA predicate rule applies and the electronic record or electronic signature is relied upon for the regulated activity.

        Binding regulation Conditional applicability
        FDA · October 2024

        Electronic Systems, Records and Signatures in Clinical Investigations

        Final FDA questions-and-answers guidance for sponsors, investigators, IRBs, CROs and other parties using electronic systems in clinical investigations.

        Regulatory guidance US clinical investigations
        EMA/INS/GCP/467532/2019

        Notice on Validation and Qualification

        Separate 2020 notice to sponsors on validation and qualification of computerised systems used in clinical trials. It is not the 2023 EMA guideline.

        Inspectorate notice Supporting EU GCP source
        EU GMP Annex 11

        Computerised Systems

        Applies to GMP-regulated activities. It may be used only as a contextual cross-GxP reference where GMP scope or an internal standard makes it applicable.

        GMP requirement Contextual for GCP-only scope
        ISPE

        GAMP 5 — Second Edition

        Industry good practice for risk-based validation, software categorisation, supplier involvement and computer software assurance approaches.

        Industry good practice Supporting interpretation
        PIC/S PI 011-3

        Good Practices for Computerised Systems in Regulated GxP Environments

        Inspectorate good-practice reference for lifecycle controls, supplier management and retirement. Direct applicability must be assessed for the activity and jurisdiction.

        Inspectorate guidance Contextual / cross-GxP
        PIC/S PI 041-1

        Good Practices for Data Management and Integrity in Regulated Environments

        Supports rationale and data-integrity depth only. Does not set severity, is not a primary GCP citation. Use to explain why an issue matters after establishing validity on GCP grounds.

        Inspectorate guidance Supporting interpretation only
        MHRA · March 2018

        GxP Data Integrity Guidance and Definitions

        Supporting data-integrity interpretation covering ALCOA+ principles, data governance and hybrid systems. Do not present it as the primary GCP computerised-systems requirement.
